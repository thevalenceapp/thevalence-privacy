Privacy Policy - Last updated: March 12, 2026

Your Privacy Matters

The Valence is designed with privacy at its core. We believe your recovery journey is deeply personal, and your data should stay that way.

Data Storage

All your personal data -- journal entries, voice memos, mood logs, reason wall items, and recovery progress -- is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data, and it is never uploaded to our servers.

AI Coach (Gemini)

When you use the AI Coach feature, your messages are sent to Google's Gemini API for processing. These messages are used solely to generate responses and are not stored by us. Google's data handling is governed by their own privacy policy. No conversation history is stored on our servers.

Third-Party Services

The Valence uses the following third-party services:

Google Gemini API -- for AI coaching responses. Google Fonts -- for typography. RevenueCat -- for subscription management (if applicable). No advertising SDKs or tracking tools are used.

What We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect your name, email, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or any personally identifiable information. We do not track your usage behavior. We do not sell or share any data with third parties for advertising purposes.

Analytics

We may collect anonymous, aggregated crash reports to improve app stability. These reports contain no personal information and cannot be traced back to individual users.

Children

The Valence is intended for users aged 17 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 17.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact
